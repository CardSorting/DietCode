# Code of Conduct: Sovereign Community Standards 🛡️

The DietCode community is a space for professional-grade orchestration and agent research. To ensure the Hive remains a welcoming and high-standard environment, all participants are expected to follow these guidelines.

---

## 1. Professional Conduct

- **Respect the Sovereignty**: Treat all contributors with professional respect, regardless of their background or experience level. 
- **Constructive Hardening**: Critique code and architectural decisions, not individuals. Focus on how to improve the Hive.
- **Collaborative Synthesis**: Share knowledge and patterns freely. The Hive grows stronger through collective intelligence.

---

## 2. Integrity of Communication

- **Clear Technical Narrative**: Communicate technical concepts clearly and authoritatively. Avoid ambiguity or "black-box" explanations.
- **Inclusive Language**: Use welcoming and inclusive language. Harassment or exclusionary behavior is strictly prohibited.

---

## 3. Enforcement

Vulnerabilities in conduct are treated with the same rigor as architectural regressions. Any behavior that compromises the community's integrity should be reported to the repository maintainers.

- **Warning**: For minor infractions, a professional reminder will be issued.
- **Exclusion**: For severe or repeated violations, participants may be removed from the Sovereign Hive community.

---
**Build Together. Grow Together. Protect the Hive.**
